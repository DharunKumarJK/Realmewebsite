WHAT'S REALME

Realme is a Chinese consumer electronics manufacturer based in Shenzhen, Guangdong.
It was founded by Li Bingzhong on May 4, 2018, who was former vice president of Oppo.
Started originally as a sub-brand of Oppo, Realme eventually ventured as its own
Realme then became the fastest-growing 5G smartphone brand with an 831% growth rate.
